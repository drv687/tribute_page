Katherine Johnson

The human "computer"

Timeline of her life

  • 1918 - born Katherine Coleman in West Virginia
  • 1936 - graduated from West Virginia State College summa cum laude with degrees in Math and French.
  • 1952 - begins working in aeronautics performing complex mathematical calculations
  • 1960s - starts working for NASA and performs the calculations that help send astronauts into space and helping John Glenn get on the moon.
  • 1986 - retires from NASA
  • 2015 - President Barack Obama awards her the Presidental Medal of Freedom
  • 2016 - Hidden Figures movie about her life and the lives of several other Black female computers is released
  • 2017 - NASA Langley names a research building after her.
  • 2020 - Passes away at age 101 in February
